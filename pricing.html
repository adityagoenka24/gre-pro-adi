The bank is actively being built — currently 160 questions are live (Percentages + Fractions). All remaining subtopics are added continuously. Pro users receive every update at no extra cost, forever.
Is this a web app or something I download?
A downloadable file that runs in your browser — no installation, no account, works offline. Open it like a website, use it like an app. Works on Windows, Mac, and mobile.
How are difficulty levels calibrated?
Each question maps to a GRE score band: Easy (130-155), Medium (155-162), Hard (162-167), Extreme Hard (167-170). Hard questions require a non-obvious insight. Extreme Hard separates 165 from 170 scorers.
What is Exam Mode?
A timed exam environment — countdown at 1.75 min/question (real GRE pace), no feedback during the test, full analysis after: accuracy by difficulty, by type, time per question, and wrong answer review.
What makes this better than Magoosh or Manhattan?
Every question shows the exact trap you fell into — not just "incorrect" but why students make that specific mistake. Built by a coach who has seen where students actually go wrong, not a content team optimizing for quantity.

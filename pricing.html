What makes this different

Most platforms show you solutions.
This one shows you why you got it wrong.

Every question in GRE Quant Pro includes a named trap — the specific cognitive error a GRE student typically makes on that exact question. Not generic advice. The precise mistake, for that precise question, explained so you recognise it next time.

⚠️ Example — Common Trap
Students see "15% of 80" and compute 15 × 80 ÷ 10 = 120, confusing percentage calculation with a ratio. The correct approach is 0.15 × 80 = 12. This specific error appears in ~60% of wrong answers on this question type.
